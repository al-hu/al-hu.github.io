Teaching and CS
Wednesday, August 31, 2016

I started my journey teaching CS at the beginning of this year, when I became a lab assistant and a junior mentor for CSM as well. Since then, I've learned a lot about teaching, and I've found that teaching is one of my passions.

Why I Started Teaching CS

I decided that volunteering as a lab assistant and junior mentor would be beneficial to me academically, since the best way to learn something is to teach it to somebody else. By helping out with the weekly labs and holding weekly tutoring sessions, I would have to review and teach the material to people who were less familiar with it - which means that I had to be solid on the foundations. In particular, since CS61A taught the core basics of computer science, I was given an opportunity to pick up where I had slacked off first semester. Ultimately, it definitely helped me hone my understanding of the material, and my students also did really well in the class.
