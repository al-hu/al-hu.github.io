5.29.16

It is often said that college is the most transformative period of your life, and freshman year has lived up to that expectation. Throughout the course of the year, I feel that I have learned and matured so much. Entering college, I had no knowledge of anything with regards to CS; I had never taken a computer science class before and did not have a definitive plan to major in it yet. Additionally, I expected college to be as easy as high school had been, despite my parents' constant warnings not to hold that mindset. As a result, I struggled for most of first semester through my courses. This was extremely alarming to me--the straight A student in highschool--, and I had never felt this sort of panic before. After the first round of midterms, I realized that a change needed to be made, and I completely revamped my study habits. By the end of the semester, I had stablized and ended up doing fine in my classes.
